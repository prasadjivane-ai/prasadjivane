About

Mr. Prasad Jivane is a Lead Software Development Engineer, AI Researcher, and Open Source Contributor with over 10+ years of experience spanning multi-cloud systems, enterprise applications, and full-stack development. He has built and scaled AI-powered platforms, no-code/low-code frameworks, and high-availability systems serving 350+ global clients.

His expertise covers cloud-native engineering (AWS, Azure, GCP, IBM), microservices architecture, serverless computing, and data engineering with technologies such as Python, Go, Django, Flask, FastAPI, PySpark, Kafka, and Kubernetes. Prasad has deep experience in designing scalable APIs, automating DevOps pipelines, and implementing security-first architectures for fintech and enterprise solutions.

As an AI researcher, he focuses on evaluation, safety, and scalable deployment of large language models (LLMs), leveraging natural language processing (NLP), retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), and model alignment to create reliable AI-driven applications. His work bridges academic research with production-grade engineering, ensuring AI systems are robust, trustworthy, and enterprise-ready.

Prasad holds an M.Tech in Computer Science from Walchand College of Engineering with research in AI & Machine Learning and Information Security on NVIDIA's Jetson TX2 platform. He has led cross-functional engineering teams, pioneered multi-tenant cloud solutions, and contributed to open-source projects advancing AI safety, developer productivity, and multi-cloud governance.

Research Interests: AI safety, LLM evaluation frameworks, retrieval-augmented generation, scalable cloud architectures, fintech AI, and multi-cloud automation.
